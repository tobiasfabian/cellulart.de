Text: Aleyna is an 11-year-old Turkish girl that lives in Berlin-Neukölln. Despite her self-confessed „chubbiness“ and „nervousness“ she is chasing the very special dream of becoming a bollywood dancer.
Little Miss Neukölln was awarded the Jena Award for Tolerance at the 2012 cellu l’art festival.
