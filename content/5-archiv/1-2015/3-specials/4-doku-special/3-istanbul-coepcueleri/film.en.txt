Text: In addition to the employees hired by the city, there are numerous unofficial garbage collectors in Istanbul who sort through the waste at night and transport everything that you can recycle to the illegal depots in their handcarts. The short documentary „İstanbul Çöpçüleri“ ( The garbage collectors of Istanbul) accompanies these man for one night and gives an insight into their lives and backgrounds.
